# Product Requirements Document: TestimonialWall - Testimonial Collection & Display Platform

## Problem Statement
Small business owners struggle to collect and display customer testimonials effectively. Existing solutions are often clunky, unattractive, and lack advanced filtering options. This makes it difficult for businesses to showcase the right testimonials at the right time, leading to missed opportunities for social proof and customer engagement.

## Goals & Success Metrics
### Goals
1. Simplify the process of collecting and displaying customer testimonials.
2. Provide a visually appealing and customizable widget for embedding testimonials on websites and social media.
3. Enable filtering of testimonials by use case to better target specific audiences.
4. Ensure user-generated content is moderated to maintain brand integrity.

### Success Metrics
1. **User Acquisition**: At least 500 small business owners sign up within the first 3 months.
2. **Engagement**: 70% of registered users actively use the widget on their websites or social media within the first month.
3. **Testimonials Collected**: Average of 10 testimonials collected per user within the first month.
4. **Satisfaction**: 90% positive user feedback on the ease of use and visual appeal of the widget.

## User Stories
1. **As a small business owner**, I want to collect video or text testimonials via a shareable link so that I can easily gather feedback from my customers.
2. **As a small business owner**, I want the app to automatically format testimonials into an embeddable widget so that I can quickly add them to my website or social media.
3. **As a small business owner**, I want to customize the design of the testimonial widget so that it matches my brand's aesthetic.
4. **As a small business owner**, I want to filter testimonials by use case so that I can showcase the most relevant ones to potential customers.
5. **As a small business owner**, I want a user-generated content moderation tool so that I can ensure all content is appropriate and aligns with my brand values.

## Functional Requirements
1. **Testimonial Collection**
   - Provide a shareable link to collect video or text testimonials.
   - Support for both video and text inputs.
   - Auto-save and categorize collected testimonials.

2. **Testimonial Formatting**
   - Automatically format collected testimonials into an embeddable widget.
   - Support for different testimonial types (video, text, image).

3. **Widget Customization**
   - Allow users to customize the design of the testimonial widget.
   - Options to change colors, fonts, and layout.
   - Preview feature to see changes in real-time.

4. **Embedding Options**
   - Provide seamless embedding options for websites and social media platforms.
   - Generate embed codes for easy integration.
   - Support for responsive design to ensure the widget looks good on all devices.

5. **Advanced Filtering**
   - Enable users to filter testimonials by use case (e.g., customer service, product quality, delivery speed).
   - Provide search functionality to quickly find specific testimonials.

6. **Content Moderation**
   - Allow business owners to review and approve user-generated content.
   - Flagging system for inappropriate or irrelevant testimonials.
   - Option to edit or delete testimonials.

## Non-Functional Requirements
1. **Performance**
   - The app should load within 3 seconds on a standard internet connection.
   - The widget should load within 2 seconds on a website.

2. **Security**
   - Implement secure protocols for storing user data and testimonials.
   - Comply with GDPR and other relevant data protection regulations.

3. **Scalability**
   - The app should be able to handle a growing number of users and testimonials without performance degradation.

4. **User Experience**
   - Ensure the app is intuitive and easy to use, with clear instructions and a clean interface.
   - Provide a help center and customer support for user assistance.
